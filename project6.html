PORTFOLIO RESUME

Ideation

We were inspired by many interesting chair designs related to visual illusion and we made some paper models to develop our own design.

Prototype

Based on our initial concept, we developed and stopped after a certain step, reflecting on the design. It was a iterative process. Finally, we finished our floating armchair. Except for the visual illusion, we referred to the famous art style in memory of Mondrian
