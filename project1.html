PORTFOLIO RESUME

Design Brief

Dongyue Web Studio has been working on the development of Tong Qu for four years. The iOS app is quite old and needs a update.

As the new leader of the design team of the studio, my goal is to create a new UI interface and improve the user experiences that help all people in Shanghai Jiao Tong University better organize and find their interested activites.

Problem Framing

We constructed the personas according to the three typical groups of our users to guide us to frame the most urgent problems we need to solve in the design process.

Concept

The new UI system of Tong Qu tries to give users a clear and intuitive interface and help them find and organize their activities more conveniently.

The new version optimizes the sections of classification and message. And it provides participators with reminder to help them take part in their own interested activities on time.
