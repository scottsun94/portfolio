Hi there! I'm Huaiwei Sun, a designer always working as the product owner, a 2nd year HCI student in Georgia Tech and a total bookaholic.

I'm passionate about transforming the newest technologies into products that really could serve people and help them achieve what they want. It's fantastic for me to see how AI and other technologies have evolved and changed this world in recent years. Apart from being curious about what values all these technologies could bring us, I also feel the responsibility to be alert to the potential dangers and risks that new technologies might bring to our societies due to our blind optimism. So I stepped into the technology industry to try to understand them deeper and ensure that they are used appropriately to contribute to this world.

I previously interned at Google and YITU. One is a tech giant while the other is a fast-growing AI unicorn. These experiences let me understand how a company's development stage could impact different aspects like the organizational structure, team collaboration, design team's structure and how the design itself should contribute to the products and the company. Another thing I learnt from both epxeriences is the importance of a good understanding of technologies and businesses for becoming a good product owner.

Who I am and where I am going have been deeply influenced by the past. Many of you have changed my life in different ways, whether it’s Steve Job’s biography at my 17 or Satre’s existentialism during my undergraduate life. I always enjoy these connections between you and me and the meaning brought by them. If you also enjoy reading, feel curious about my work or just have something to share with me during your trip in my little space, please feel free to contact me.

Let this connection impact both you and me.
